02 The Problem

THE COMPLIANCE
GAP IS A LIABILITY.

Reports ≠ Communication

ESG reports are written for regulators, not people. They're dense, technical, and impossible to turn into credible public communication without expert intervention.

48% of companies lack standard ESG metrics

Marketing Tools Create Risk

Existing content tools optimise for persuasion, not compliance. Using them for ESG communication is a legal liability — not a communications strategy.

Regulators moving from guidance to enforcement in 2025

ESG is Now a Risk Function

After EU Omnibus reform, scrutiny is higher than ever. One misleading ESG claim can trigger legal action, media backlash, and investor distrust simultaneously.
